Festivals

St. Antun is celebrated every 13. June First of all, people antend the holy mass in church and after that many families have a feast.

St. Ana is celebrated every July 26. St. Ana is celebrated in the village of Knez Gorica. But everyone from Vukmanic attends it also. And vice versa too. The only difference with St. Ana is that the feast is on the first Sunday after July 26.

Traditions

Also once a year (mostly in winter) people gather around the families that own pigs to attend a pig-slaughter. The pig will be then processed to into further edible products.

Some people own vineyards. They harvest the grapes and make homemade wine. Every Family does something different in the wine making process which makes every families wine unique and recognisable.
